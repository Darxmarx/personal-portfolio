About Me

Ever since I was but a young boy, technology always fascinated me. From the simple phones around the house, to exposure to my very first video game console on my family's battered television set, my interest in tech only grew alongside my own body. Today, after not only admiring the physical technology, but the coding running everything under the hood, I eagerly improve my full-stack web development skills. Full of energy and passion, I am an individual with a flexible, growth mindset and an inherent eagerness to learn. Motivated to improve myself for the sake of helping others, I strive to create useful applications that better everyone's lives whenever they call upon me.
